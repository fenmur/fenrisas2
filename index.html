About Me

Welcome to my world of visual storytelling! I'm Fenris Murshud, a 26-year-old student delving into the realm of New Media Production and Design at SAIT in picturesque Calgary, Canada. Since childhood, I've been captivated by the art of photography, fascinated by its ability to immortalize moments and narrate stories without uttering a single word. However, it was at SAIT where my passion for photography truly ignited into an inferno of creativity.
