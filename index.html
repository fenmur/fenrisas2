About Me

Welcome to my world of visual storytelling! I'm Fenris Murshud, a 26-year-old student delving into the realm of New Media Production and Design at SAIT in picturesque Calgary, Canada. Since childhood, I've been captivated by the art of photography, fascinated by its ability to immortalize moments and narrate stories without uttering a single word. However, it was at SAIT where my passion for photography truly ignited into an inferno of creativity. Beyond being a mere educational institution, SAIT has become a crucible of creativity for meâ€”a place where I've been molded into a visual storyteller with an unyielding hunger for innovation and an insatiable thirst for exploration. Surrounded by like-minded individuals and fueled by cutting-edge techniques, my journey into the depths of visual storytelling took flight. Through the lens of my camera, I embark on voyages into worlds within worlds. Whether it's the enchanting allure of the golden hour enveloping rugged mountains or the serene tranquility of a placid lake, I endeavor to capture the essence of every fleeting moment. Join me as I share the stories behind my photos and explore the beauty of the world through my lens.

More about myself and my photos

I have tried many editing apps in the past, but to this day, the best of them is Adobe Lightroom, both for PC and mobile. Usually, I use mobile version, where I create presets with editing settings, save them, and use them for the future photos.

I use Samsung Galaxy S20+ camera. However, I am saving up for a professional camera.

I plan to keep photography as a side hobby, or as a side part-time job for additional money. However, the more I work with camera, the more I understand that possibly I want to make a small business out of it, mainly specilazing in pets photos.
